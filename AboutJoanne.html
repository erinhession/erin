Joanne Hynes has a highly personal, unconventional approach to fashion. Since

graduating from Central Saint Martins MA in 2001 (where her obsession with costume

jewellery began) and establishing her label in 2003, Hynes has created an

immediately identifiable signature with her rebellious ready-to-wear clothing and

accessory lines.

 

Joanne Hynes’ partnership with Dunnes Stores launched in September 2016. Hynes

embraces the muscle and pace of the high street, while utilising her design and

craft expertise to create an ambitious and fully comprehensive designer offering.

Every piece within the collection is designed to stand alone as a statement of

intent – an offering of more for women who seek to express rather than impress.

 

The label has shown internationally and sold within the world’s most renowned luxury

retailers. Previous collaborations include Pretty Polly hosiery (2012) and Joanne

Hynes for Topshop (2006). Joanne returned to Ireland in 2012 and lives in Wicklow

with her partner and five young children.
